Trail Monitoring System

The Trail Monitoring System in Kitchener, ON is an IoT project showcasing technology to capture weather and soil condition data and let users know the condition of the trails. The system answers the question, "Are the trails muddy?", informs users if the trails are open or closed via trail remote controlled open/close signs and an IOS app, and provide usage data on trail users to answer the question "How busy are they and who is using them?".
